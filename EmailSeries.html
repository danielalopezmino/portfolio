Email Series


Product: TAPinto Email series
Role: UX/UI Designer
Users: Readers TAPinto

Context

TAPinto is a local news platform that connects town editors with their communities.
For many readers, email is the main way they experience TAPinto:
- A welcome when they subscribe
- Weekly or daily content updates
- Local alerts
- Stories that explain what TAPinto is and why it matters
- Gentle prompts for engagement or donations
Editors create content on the platform, but the platform is responsible for how that content reaches readers. That makes email a product surface — not just a communication channel.

The Problem

Before this work, email communication lacked structure and intention.
Different types of emails — welcome messages, content updates, alerts, and informational emails — existed, but:
- They didn’t feel connected to each other
- They didn’t guide new readers through the experience
- They didn’t help editors build a consistent relationship with their audience

The Design Challenge

The challenge was to design email series that feel like a conversation, not a broadcast.
The system needed to:
- Support first-time readers discovering the platform
- Help people set preferences and choose what they care about
- Tell the story of TAPinto and its role in the community
- Enable editors to send regular content without friction
- Scale across hundreds of towns without losing personality

A Key Design Decision

Instead of focusing on single messages, I designed email journeys, each one tied to a reader’s moment in time.
Each series answers a different need:
- Welcome & onboarding — what TAPinto is and how it works
- Preference setup — what kind of content the reader wants
- Platform storytelling — why local journalism matters
- Engagement & donations — supporting the community
- Content delivery — daily, weekly, or alert-based emails
- Every email has a clear role inside a larger story.

The Solution

I designed a flexible email system that allows:
- Editors to focus on content
- Readers to receive relevant, timely information
- The platform to ensure consistency and clarity

The system includes:

- Clearly defined email series by purpose
- Content blocks that support storytelling, not just links
- Clear patterns for newsletters, alerts, and informational emails
